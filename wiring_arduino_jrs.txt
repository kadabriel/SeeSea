Arduino Uno 5V test (JSN-SR20-Y1, trig/echo)

Connections:
- JSN VCC  -> Arduino 5V
- JSN GND  -> Arduino GND
- JSN TRIG -> Arduino D9 (digital output)
- JSN ECHO -> Arduino D8 (digital input, 5V tolerant)

No level shifting needed on Uno (5V IO). Remove any series/level resistors used for ESP32 and wire directly for this test.

Baud rate for test sketch: 115200 bps.
